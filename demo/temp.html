About LK Emerald Beach

LK Emerald Beach is located next to Pattaya-Naklua beach overlooking the most beautiful Gulf of Thailand which it is very suitable for relaxation escape of all visitors. Moreover, hotel is nearest the best of shopping center in Pattaya such as Terminal 21, Central Festival Pattaya Beach, Central Marina Shopping Mall and etc. It is only 1 kilometer away from Pattaya bus terminal which easily for travellers and it takes about 90 minutes from Suvarnabhumi airport and approximately 45 minutes from U-tapao ...
